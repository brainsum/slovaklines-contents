About the magazine:


The In drive Slovak Lines magazine is an on-board magazine of Slovak Lines, so you will find it mainly on the coaches (on the Bratislava - Vienna line). However, you may also see it at the premises of the Mlynské nivy coach station in Bratislava and other interesting places.
It provides for you the latest information in the area of traveling, tourism, sport, information technologies, wellness, art as well as profiles of interesting personalities and interviews with them.
The magazine is here for you, dear passengers. Its task is to make your traveling and waiting for your coach more pleasurable as well as to provide you with a lot of interesting information.

The magazine offers unique media-advertising space.
Would you like to promote some products or services? We will be happy to send you all necessary information.

You just need to write us to the address: marketing@slovaklinesmagazin.sk
